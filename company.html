About Company

AM DATA LAB PVT LTD.


AM DATA LAB is a leading platform based drone survey,incorporated company operating in Pune,since 2020. We automate manual processes to make your operations and maintenance tasks efficient, expedient and economical.

Our services include high-precision Drone Surveying and Mapping, Visual and Thermal Inspections for Solar plants, Transmission lines, Wind Turbines, Thermal plants, Factories, Dams, and Hydropower Projects. We provide real-time construction progress monitoring for Highways, Railways, Metro Projects, and other infrastructure developments. Additionally, we conduct post-disaster surveys for rapid assessment and damage evaluation, as well as Videography services for Oil and Gas Pipelines, construction sites, and general insurance assets.

By leveraging Drone technology and a team of experts, AM Data Lab Pvt Ltd delivers reliable, innovative, and cost-effective solutions. Our commitment is to help industries improve operational efficiency, ensure safety, and achieve greater accuracy in data-driven decision-making.
