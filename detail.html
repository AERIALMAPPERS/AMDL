About Company


AM DATA LAB is a leading platform based drone survey,incorporated company operating in Pune,since 2015. We automate manual processes to make your operations and maintenance tasks efficient, expedient and economical.

Our services include drone surveying, mapping, visual and thermal inspection to solar plants, transmission lines & utilities, wind turbines, thermal plants, factories, dams and hydro power projects, telecom towers, coal & mine. We also offer construction progress monitoring services, post-disaster surveys and videography services for highways, rail and metro projects, oil and gas pipelines and plants, construction sites and general insurance assets.
