Vision & Mission of Company

AM DATA LAB PVT LTD.

AM DATA LAB PVT LTD is a family owned and operated, incorporated company operating in Pune, since 2020. AMDL is a LIDAR service company built on teamwork and our love for technology. AMDL specializes in low altitude, high definition aerial imaging services, including aerial photography and aerial Videography.
We operate state of the art Unmanned Aerial Vehicle Systems: for video and HD photography, long endurance UAV for GIS- based mapping, Thermal inspection and surveying projects.
We offer a cost effective alternative to manned aircraft for a variety of applications in the fields of engineering, construction, mining, city planning and monitoring, agriculture, archeology, Solar Farms, Wind Turbines and transmission lines.



Vision

Our Vision Is To Utilize Aerial Intelligence to assess and evaluate earth . Geographical evolution by leveraging aerial data and Intelligent analysis,
We aim to support sustainable Development enhance environmental monitoring and contribute to the progressive advancement of advance technology
